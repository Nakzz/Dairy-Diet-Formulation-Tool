Additional Information Displayed

Note that additional information is displayed, after solving, at the end of the tool:

·The predicted nutrient costs ($/unit) at the bottom of each nutrient column, and

·The “prediction accuracy” represented by the R-square and the Adjusted R-square, which indicate the level of prediction accuracy of a particular analysis.
