Calculations

 

 

 

Analyze Button

The tool is ready to perform the calculations by simply hitting the "Analyze" button on top of the table. After hitting this button, individual nutrient predicted costs in each column, values for the predicted prices, and actual price as % of predicted price in the right most section of the table appear.

 

 

Actual prices as % of Predicted Values

Actual prices as % of predicted value GREATER than 100 (which appear highlighted in red) indicate OVERPRICED ingredients and actual prices as % of predicted value LESSER than 100 (which appear highlighted in green) indicate UNDERPRICED ingredients. Therefore, the lesser this value (last column of the table), the more that ingredient is a BARGAIN. When the ingredient is not part of the solution, but it has a market price the hightligths for "overpriced" and "underpriced" are sligtly different in tone color. Ingredients that do not have a market price will have only their predicted value displayed.

 

Results and Additional Analyses

Study the results and perform additional analyses. Note that every time a change is made, results disappear because a re-calculation (pressing the "Analyze" button) is required.

 

 

Download Results as Spreadsheet

Values in the table can be downloaded to a spreadsheet format by hitting the "Download Results" button. This spreadsheet will have exactly the same headings and values as seen.
